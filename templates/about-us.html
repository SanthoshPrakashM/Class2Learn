What?
WE DO

Class 2 Learn – eGrove Systems’ Brainchild

  • eGrove Systems is an educational technology company backed by a team of experienced educators who have created lessons, learning materials, course models and exams through extensive research and quality curriculum design with a focus on student engagement.
  • Class 2 Learn was designed to make online learning and homeschooling accessible to all students.
  • Our goal is to connect students and teachers and make learning easier by offering finely tuned courses in the form of ebooks, videos, slides and reference materials.
  • Class 2 Learn uses SCORM-compliant packages to create learning materials. Teachers can manage courses and easily add content while controlling what students can access.
  • Using a custom assessment workflow model, students can take assessments, measure their progress with quick and easy assessments, and provide actionable data about their classroom experience.
  • Video-based self-paced courses are also available for students.
  • We also provide resources to help students find information about scholarships offered by other organizations and institutions.
  • We also work with educational institutions and government agencies to establish, organize, and maintain online platforms for K-12 learning and assessment.

Class 2 Learn Unique Features:

  • AI-powered personalized learning paths that adapt to each student’s performance, ensuring efficient and customized progress.
  • Interactive and engaging content with videos, quizzes, and gamified modules to enhance understanding and retention.
  • Real-time progress tracking and analytics for students, parents, and educators to monitor performance and identify areas for improvement.
  • Expert-curated curriculum aligned with academic standards, delivering high-quality and relevant learning material.
  • Smart assessments with instant feedback, detailed insights, and improvement suggestions for better outcomes.
  • Seamless access across mobile, tablet, and desktop, enabling learning anytime and anywhere.
  • Live classes with experienced educators and instant doubt resolution for deeper concept clarity.
  • Gamified learning experience with rewards, badges, and milestones to keep students motivated.
  • Dedicated parent and teacher dashboards providing clear insights into student performance and engagement.
  • Secure and scalable platform built with modern technology for smooth, reliable, and safe learning experiences.
