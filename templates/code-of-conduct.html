Code of Conduct

Our code of conduct outlines the expectations for all users of the Class2Learn platform.

Guidelines

We are committed to providing a safe and secure online environment for all users. Our guidelines include:

  • Respectful Communication: All users are expected to communicate with respect and professionalism.
  • Non-Discrimination: We do not tolerate discrimination or harassment of any kind.
  • Intellectual Property: We respect the intellectual property rights of all users.
  • Privacy: We respect the privacy of all users and protect their personal information.
  • Compliance: All users are expected to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
