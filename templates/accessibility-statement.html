Accessibility Statement

  • This accessibility statement outlines our commitment to providing an inclusive and accessible experience for all users.
  • We strive to ensure that our platform is usable by people with disabilities.

Accessibility Commitment

At Class2learn, we are dedicated to creating a digital environment that is accessible and inclusive for everyone, regardless of ability. We adhere to recognized accessibility standards, such as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, to ensure our website meets diverse user needs.

Feedback on Accessibility

Your feedback is essential to our ongoing accessibility efforts. If you experience any accessibility issues while navigating our website, please reach out to us at contact@class2learn.com. Be sure to include details about the specific page or resource involved so we can promptly address the issue.
